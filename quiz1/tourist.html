Tourist Places

Malang is a favorite destination for people when the holidays arrive. There are so many tourist attractions in Malang, from themes park, mountains, waterfalls and beaches. If you want to vacation in Malang, it is not enough for a day to visit all the tourist attractions that exist. At least 3 days for a vacation in Malang. The following are examples of tourist attractions in Malang that are most in demand by people and most visited.

  • First picture is Jatim Park Group. Jatim park group is a place for entertainment and education tourism. The Jatim Park group consists of Jatim Park 1, Jatim Park 2, Jatim Park 3, a transport museum, BNS, and predator fun park. All of these places have their own themes and characteristics.
  • Second Picture is Bromo Mountain. This mountain is actually bordered by 4 districts, namely Malang, Probolinggo, Pasuruan, and Lumajang. This mountain has a height of 2329 meters above sea level. As a tourist attraction, Bromo is attractive because of its status as an active volcano. Mount Bromo is included in the Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park area. Mount Bromo's body shape interlocks between valleys and canyons with a caldera or sea of sand covering an area of about 10 square kilometers. It has a crater with a diameter of ± 800 meters (north-south) and ± 600 meters (east-west). While the danger area is a circle with a radius of 4 km from the center of the Bromo crater.
  • The beaches in South Malang are very numerous, such as Balekambang Beach, Batu Bengkung Beach, Sendiki Beach, etc. Like most beaches, Malang Selatan beach offers views of the southern sea with big waves that are very beautiful and refreshing to the eye
  • Gunung Banyak is actually a plateau located in Songgokerto, East Java which has an altitude of about 1,315 meters. Gunung Banyak is known as a place to do paragliding. From the top of Mount Banyak, you will see views of Batu City and Malang City, if you look to the north you will see Mount Welirang and to the south you can see Mount Panderman, even if the weather is clear, the peak of Mahameru Mount Semeru can also be seen in the distance.
