Malang City

Malang City is a beautiful city. Malang city is located in East Java, Indonesia. What makes it beautiful is because of its geographic location. Malang City, which is located at an altitude between 440 - 667 meters above sea level, is one of the tourist destinations in East Java because of its natural potential and climate. Located in the middle of Malang Regency, astronomically it is 112.06 째 - 112.07 째 East Longitude and 7.06 째 - 8.02 째 South Latitude. And surrounded by mountains: Arjuno to the north, Mount Semeru in the east, Mount Kawi and Panderman in the West, Mount Kelud in the south.
