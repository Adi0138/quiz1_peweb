Foods

Malang is one of the culinary paradises in East Java. From heavy meals to snacks, all are available in Malang.
The following are examples of typical Malang foods that are most in demand by local residents and migrants alike.

  • First picture is Bakso or we can call it meatball. Bakso is a food that has become an icon of Malang. If you are going to Malang, you must try this dish. Bakso Malang is the most delicious meatball in Indonesia
  • Second picture is Ketan. Ketan is one of traditional food in Indonesia. You can easily find ketan at Malang, just search on google maps "Pos Ketan Legenda". That place sells ketan with various flavors. So delicious.
  • Third picture is Cwimie. Cwimie is actually like chicken noodle but with a different touch. The taste is very distinctive and so delicious.
  • Fourth picture is Sate Kelinci. It's same with sate ayam or sate kambing. The textures is overall same, but the colour of sate kelinci is different with sate ayam, the colour is white reddish. Sate kelinci apparently has a much lower fat content than sate ayam.
